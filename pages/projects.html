Simple Stocks Simulator

While taking the course MIT Web.lab in the winter 2021, my team and I created a simple stock market simulator for the beginners. Using real historical market data, we designed a simulating stock market to help investors learn about quantitative analysis. ReactJS, Node, and MongoDB were mainly utilized throughout the project.
